wedding vows meaning

for better, for worse

They stay together no matter what happens in their life, help each other, support each other in difficult situations. Be happy for each other in good times

for richer, for poorer

They will still live even if they don't have money at all. They will still stay together if for example one of them as lots of money

in sickness and in health

They will stay together even if one of them got sick and wiil look after each other. For example, make drinks or food and bring it to the bed to the ill husband or wife

to love and cherish

To love, protect, respect and show gratitude

till death do us part

live together until someone dies
